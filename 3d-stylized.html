Stylized Character Design

3D MODELLING.

During my final year in Digital Media at RMIT, we were given a chance to choose our own project to do. This project was to challenge myself into exploring the making of a stylized character like you would see in Pixar movies or games like Overwatch. It was also to challenge my skills in sculpting in Zbrush and see what I can come up with. Concept and sketches were done on paper before.
