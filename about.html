About

What do I do?

My Name is CEDRIC.

I have been working on the Design field for about 5 years now, currently building my career towards freelancing and business.

My Work

I have an undergraduate degree in Digital Media and postgraduate degree in Business and IT from RMIT, in Melbourne Australia. Digital Media ignited my passion in design and taught me about finding beauty in things. My degree in Business gave me purpose and taught me to put my works to a better use. Since then, I have worked for several organizations and startups and help them grow, by translating ideas into visuals, and attracting customers. I have also learned through several competitions, such as Hackathons, to work fast and make designs accessible and easy to use. I am still learning every day, and every day I am learning about transferrable skills: In what other area can I apply my learning to do something unique?

Animated Explainers

Animated Explainer are short videos used to explain the basics behind an idea or a value proposition for a business. All illustrations and animations are made using Adobe Illustrator and After Effects.

Web/Mobile UI Design

I create responsive, elegant and slick designs for your websites or mobile apps. I also do front-end web development using HTML, CSS and JS. Also include email templates.

Graphic Design

I come up with beautiful design layouts and concepts for graphic design works, for printing or fully digital, including: Brochures, flyers, logos, banners, magazines and posters.
