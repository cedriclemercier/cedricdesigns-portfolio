REMIX SERIES

he relationship between what we see and what we know is never resolved, is never equal.

To illustrate the sentence above, I made a series of images on the theme of politics and environment. I wanted to illustrate the fact that what we see happening all around the world is not necessarily the truth. We may know what is happening, but we are seeing only a portion of the truth behind it because it has been filthered by the medias. On the image, we see somebody held prisoner in a devastated planet, looking at good things happening on the TV while underneath his body is a pile of trash that is about the crumble.
